Who we are

A student‑run cultural organization connecting Bangladeshi students and friends of Bangladesh across Rowan University.

Rowan Bangladesh Student Association (BSA) is a home away from home for Bangladeshi and Bangladeshi‑American students, creating space to share our traditions while supporting one another through college life.

We organize cultural nights, socials, and collaborative programs that highlight Bangladeshi food, music, fashion, and history while helping new students find community on campus.

You’ll see us at the Student Center, in classrooms, and at campus‑wide events — hosting performances, serving food, cheering at games, and representing Bangladesh wherever we go.

What we do throughout the year

  • Cultural events showcasing Bangladeshi music, dance, and traditional wear.
  • Welcome sessions and orientation programs for new and transfer students.
  • Study sessions, mentorship, and informal check‑ins during busy semesters.
  • Sports, game nights, movie nights, and late‑night food hangouts.
  • Collaborations with other multicultural and international student groups.

Whether you want to perform, help organize, or just show up and enjoy, there’s always a place for you in BSA.
